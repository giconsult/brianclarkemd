Procedures We Perform

Dr. Clarke and his friendly, experienced medical staff will walk you through each step of your procedure. Dr. Clarke has helped pioneer many of the procedures used by physicians in the GI industry, and our team will make your visit as comfortable as possible.

  • Upper endoscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Therapeutic ERCP
  • Bariatric GI endoscopy
  • Diagnosis and treatment of inflammatory bowel disease (IBS)
  • Hepatology
  • Evaluation and treatment of acute and chronic liver diseases

If you have been scheduled for a procedure and need preparation information, please contact our office and a staff member will get you the information you need. If you are a new patient, please complete the New Patient Information Form and return it to our office.
