Who We Are

We are an experienced gastroenterology consulting firm led by Dr. Brian Clarke, specializing in the evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment of the GI tract.

Our services include:

  • Diagnosis and treatment of inflammatory bowel disease (IBS)
  • Evaluation and treatment of acute and chronic liver diseases
  • Videocapsule endoscopy
  • Upper endoscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Therapeutic ERCP
  • Bariatric GI endoscopy
  • Hepatology

Our staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and understands advanced diagnostic and therapeutic GI procedures.
